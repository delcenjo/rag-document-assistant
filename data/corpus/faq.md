# Nimbus Notes — Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I export my notes?

Notes can be exported from Settings > Export in Markdown, PDF or HTML format,
either individually or per notebook.

## Can I use Nimbus Notes offline?

Yes. The desktop and mobile apps cache your notes locally and synchronise
automatically the next time you are online. The web app requires a connection.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel subscription. You keep access to paid features
until the end of the current billing period, after which the account reverts to
the Free plan.

## How do I delete my account?

Settings > Account > Delete account. Your data is permanently purged 30 days
after deletion.

## Which languages are supported?

The interface is available in 12 languages, including English, Spanish, French,
German and Japanese.
