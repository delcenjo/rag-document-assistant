# Nimbus Notes — Pricing

Nimbus Notes offers four plans. All paid plans include a 14-day free trial of
Pro features, and annual billing saves roughly two months compared to monthly.

## Free

- Up to 3 notebooks and 1 GB of storage.
- Core editing and search.
- Community support only.

## Pro — $8 per month (or $80 per year)

- Unlimited notebooks and 50 GB of storage.
- 30 days of version history.
- Optional end-to-end encryption.
- Priority email support.

## Team — $15 per user per month

- Everything in Pro, plus shared workspaces and admin controls.
- 200 GB of storage per user.
- Single sign-on (SSO).

## Enterprise — custom pricing

- SAML SSO, audit logs and a dedicated account manager.
- Optional on-premise deployment.

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from Settings; access continues until
the end of the current billing period.
